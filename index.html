Real-World Mobile Image Denoising Dataset with Efficient Baselines

A curated dataset that captures the complexity of mobile noise patterns alongside strong baseline models optimized for on-device deployment. The work sets a new foundation for realistic benchmarking and rapid experimentation in mobile imaging pipelines.

ArXiv 2025 • Medical Imaging

Bone Substructure Contours for 2D/3D Registration

Introducing contour-based representations that dramatically improve registration robustness, enabling precise alignment between intraoperative X-rays and 3D anatomical models. The approach accelerates surgical planning and enhances guidance quality in orthopaedic workflows.
